Frequently Asked Questions

What is CosmicLens Core?
CosmicLens Core is an all-in-one education operations platform that helps teams manage scheduling, communication, instructor-student assignments, and performance tracking in one central dashboard.
How does it help my organization?
It reduces the time spent on admin tasks like manual scheduling and communication by automating instructor assignments, student tracking, and lesson management — so you can focus more on teaching and less on logistics.
Do I need technical skills to use it?
Not at all. CosmicLens Core is built to be intuitive. If you know how to manage a calendar or send an email, you’ll feel right at home. No technical setup or configuration required.
Can I use it for multiple programs or locations?
Yes. You can manage multiple programs, departments, or even physical locations under a single account with permission-based access for each team.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Only users with specific permissions can access sensitive information, and regular backups ensure nothing is ever lost.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. You can start using CosmicLens Core for free with access to all core features. No credit card needed — just sign up and explore.
